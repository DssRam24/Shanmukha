000 — Origin

I was the quiet one. Bullied sometimes, sick often — sinusitis made breathing a battle, but my mind never stopped running. Cricket was the escape. A bat in hand felt lighter than whatever I was carrying that day.

At home, curiosity ran the house. While other kids played games, my brother and I dismantled 10 to 15 computers just to see what lived inside them. Not because we knew what we were doing — because we needed to know how things worked. That question never really left me.

Somewhere in between, I discovered art — hours in MS Paint, bad sketches, weird color palettes, zero regrets. That side grew up with me. Today I design, build, and ship. I mix technology with creativity because I never liked choosing only one.

Now I'm focused on building things that are mine. Not just products — a life that reflects who I actually am.

Creative. Curious. Restless. Always building. ✦
